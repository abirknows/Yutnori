Yutnori

How to play
  • Throw the sticks each turn — Do (1), Gae (2), Geol (3), Yut (4 ★), Mo (5 ★). ★ throws earn another go.
  • Bring a new piece in from Start, or move one already on the board.
  • Land on your own piece(s) to stack — they move together from then on.
  • Land on a rival to send its whole stack back to Start and earn an extra throw.
  • ★ corners and the center are shortcut junctions — cutting through gets you Home faster.
  • Back-Do (optional rule) moves a board piece backward one step.
  • First player to bring all 4 pieces Home wins.
